### 👾 Who Am I?

I'm **Siddhant Jena** — a builder who thrives at the intersection of **hardware and software**, from ESP32 microcontrollers capturing spatial telemetry to Next.js dashboards rendering it in real time.

- 🛰️ **Currently building:** `TempMent` & `Proxima` — advanced IoT telemetry platforms
- 🧠 **Deep diving into:** AI inference pipelines & microservice mesh architectures
- 🤝 **Collaborate with me on:** Open-source IoT, Web3 & Agritech
- ⚡ **My obsession:** Shaving 50ms off an API response and calling it a victory
- 🌍 **Philosophy:** *Ship fast. Iterate faster. Never ship broken.*
- 📬 **Reach me:** [worksiddhant18@gmail.com](mailto:worksiddhant18@gmail.com)
